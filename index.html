Eggie's Functions

Eggie is the baby of Mr. Engineering and Mrs. Arts.

From the beginning, we wanted to create a light that is both beautiful and versatile. We are proud to present Eggie - the marriage of art and engineering. Its glow will warm any room and its presence will light up any occasion.

Battery Life Up To 7 Hours

With a battery life of over 7 hours, Eggie is perfect for one nightâ€™s sleep.

Wireless Charging

Its wireless charging function allows you to easily plop it on or off to charge.

Sustainability

Eggie is designed to be sustainable. It only consumes 2.5 Watt and it will stop charging once it is fully charged.

Shake To Turn On/Off

You may also switch Eggie off by shaking it twice, and simply repeat to turn it back on.

Water Resistant

Its water resistance function allows Eggie to float on water, opening up a multitude of different uses.

Create Your Own

The texture of Eggie's shell also makes the perfect canvas for artists, couples, friends, and children to express themselves. With a marker, Eggie can be the platform of your next dream.
